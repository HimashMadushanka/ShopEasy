Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns and quick refunds for your peace of mind

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your ShopEasy purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

30-Day Return Policy: You have 30 days from the delivery date to return most items for a full refund.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • All original tags and packaging must be attached
  • Items must be in resalable condition
  • Return shipping label must be used (if provided)

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

1

Start Your Return

Log in to your account and visit the "Order History" section. Select the item you wish to return.

2

Print Return Label

Print the prepaid return shipping label that will be emailed to you after initiating the return.

3

Package Your Item

Securely package the item in its original packaging and attach the return label to the outside.

4

Drop Off Package

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location or schedule a pickup.

Refund Information

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund quickly:

  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days after we receive your return
  • Refund Method: Refunds are issued to your original payment method
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return Shipping: Free for defective or incorrect items

Note: It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer easy exchanges:

  • Exchanges are subject to product availability
  • Contact us within 14 days of delivery to request an exchange
  • You may be asked to return the original item first
  • We'll ship the replacement item once we receive your return
